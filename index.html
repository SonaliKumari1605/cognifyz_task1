Motion

picture credit: vedantu

The change of position with respect to its surrounding is known as motion.

Laws Of Motion

First Law Of Motion

When an object is rest it will remain in rest, when an object is in motion it will remain in motion unless and until an external force is apllied on them.

Second Law Of Motion

The rate of change of momentum is directly propertional to force and the object will move in the direction in which the force is applied.

Third Law Of Motion

Every action there is equal and opposite reaction.

Equation Of Motion

First Equation of Motion

v = u+at
where; 'v' is the final velocity of the object, 'u' is the intial velocity of the object, 'a' is the acceleration and 't' is the time.

Second Equation Of Motion

s = ut+(at^2)/2
where; 's' is the distance, 'u' is the intial velocity,'t' is the time and 'a' is the acceleration.

Third Equation Of Motion

v^2 = u^2+2as
where; 'v' is the final velocity, 'u' is the initial velocity, 'a' is the acceleration and 's' is the distance.
